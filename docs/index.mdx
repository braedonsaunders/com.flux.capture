Flux Capture transforms how your business processes vendor bills, invoices, and expense documents in NetSuite. Using advanced AI and machine learning, Capture automatically extracts data from your documents and creates transactions with minimal manual effort.

## What Flux Capture Does

Simply upload an invoice or forward it via email, and Flux Capture will:

- **Extract all key data** - Vendor name, invoice number, dates, amounts, and line items
- **Match to your vendors** - Automatically find the right vendor in NetSuite
- **Detect problems** - Flag duplicates, suspicious amounts, and data issues
- **Learn your preferences** - Get smarter with every document you process

## Key Benefits

### Save Time
Stop manually entering invoice data. Flux Capture reads your documents and populates all fields automatically, reducing data entry time by up to 80%.

### Reduce Errors
Human data entry is error-prone. AI extraction eliminates typos, transposed numbers, and missed information.

### Catch Issues Early
Built-in fraud detection identifies duplicate invoices, unusual amounts, and potential problems before they become costly mistakes.

### Improve Over Time
The learning engine remembers your corrections and vendor preferences, getting more accurate with every invoice you process.

## How It Works

1. **Upload** - Drag and drop invoices, or forward them to your dedicated email address
2. **Extract** - AI reads the document and extracts vendor, amounts, dates, and line items
3. **Review** - Check the extracted data in a side-by-side view with the original document
4. **Approve** - One click creates the vendor bill in NetSuite

## Requirements

- NetSuite account with SuiteScript 2.1 support
- User role with permissions to create vendor bills and view file cabinet
- OCR/AI provider credentials for the provider you choose
