Flux Capture requires a valid license to operate. This guide explains how to obtain, activate, and manage your license.

## Getting a License

### New Customers

To get started with Flux Capture:

1. Visit [fluxfornetsuite.com](https://fluxfornetsuite.com)
2. Request a demo or contact sales
3. Receive your license key via email

### Existing Customers

If you already have a Flux account:

1. Log into the Flux customer portal
2. Navigate to your subscriptions
3. Copy your Capture license key

## Activating Your License

### Step 1: Open Settings

1. Launch Flux Capture in NetSuite
2. Click the **Settings** icon in the navigation
3. Scroll to the **License** section

### Step 2: Enter Your License Key

1. Paste your license key in the License Key field
2. Click **Save Settings**
3. Wait for validation (usually 2-3 seconds)

### Step 3: Verify Activation

After saving, you should see:
- A green checkmark indicating valid license
- Your subscription status (Active)
- Your license expiration date

> ✅ **Tip**: If validation fails, double-check that you copied the entire license key without extra spaces.

## License Types

### Standard License

The standard Flux Capture license includes:
- Unlimited document processing
- All extraction features (Smart Extract)
- Fraud detection (Fraud Shield)
- Machine learning (Learning Engine)
- Email-to-Invoice capability
- Side-by-side review interface

### Trial License

Trial licenses are available for evaluation:
- Full feature access
- Limited time period (typically 14-30 days)
- No document volume limits during trial

## License Validation

Flux Capture validates your license periodically:

- **On startup** - Checked when you open the application
- **Hourly** - Background validation while in use
- **Daily** - Full re-validation each day

### Offline Operation

If your NetSuite instance temporarily cannot reach the Flux license server:

- A **24-hour grace period** allows continued operation
- Normal features remain available during the grace period
- Reconnection automatically restores full validation

> ℹ️ **Info**: The grace period ensures your operations aren't disrupted by temporary network issues.

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing License Status

To check your current license status:

1. Go to **Settings** in Flux Capture
2. Look for the License section
3. View your status, expiration date, and features

### Renewing Your License

Before your license expires:

1. Contact your Flux account representative
2. Or renew through the customer portal at [fluxfornetsuite.com](https://fluxfornetsuite.com)
3. Your existing license key may be extended, or you'll receive a new one

### Updating Your License Key

If you receive a new license key:

1. Go to **Settings** in Flux Capture
2. Replace the old license key with the new one
3. Click **Save Settings**
4. Verify the new expiration date appears

## Troubleshooting License Issues

### "License Required" Error

If you see this error:

1. **Check your license key** - Verify it's entered correctly
2. **Refresh the license** - Click the refresh button in Settings
3. **Check expiration** - Your license may have expired
4. **Contact support** - If the issue persists

### License Not Validating

Common causes and solutions:

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Typo in license key | Re-copy and paste carefully |
| Expired license | Renew your subscription |
| Network issues | Wait and try again later |
| Account mismatch | Contact support to verify account |

### Expired License

When your license expires:

1. You'll see a notification in Flux Capture
2. Document processing will be disabled
3. Existing data remains accessible
4. Renew to restore full functionality

## Transferring Licenses

### Moving to a New Account

If you're migrating to a new NetSuite account:

1. Contact support@fluxfornetsuite.com
2. Provide your current and new account IDs
3. We'll issue a license for the new account

### Sandbox Accounts

Development and sandbox accounts:

1. Sandbox instances typically need their own license
2. Contact your Flux representative for sandbox licensing options
3. Enter the sandbox license key in your sandbox environment
